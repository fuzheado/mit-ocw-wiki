# Dynamic Leadership: Using Improvisation in Business

The first two weeks of this course are an overview of performing improvisation with introductory and advanced exercises in the techniques of improvisation.&nbsp;The final four weeks focus on applying these concepts in business situations to practice and mastering these improvisation tools in leadership learning.
