# Introduction to Drama

Drama combines the literary arts of storytelling and poetry with the world of live performance.&nbsp;As a form of ritual as well as entertainment, drama has served to unite communities and challenge social norms, to vitalize and disturb its audiences.&nbsp;In order to understand this rich art form more fully, we will study and discuss a sampling of plays that exemplify different kinds of dramatic structure; class members will also participate in, attend, and review dramatic performances.
