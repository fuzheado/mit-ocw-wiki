# Spanish II

Spanish II continues to develop students’ listening, speaking, reading and writing skills using the second part of the&nbsp;video-based program, Destinos, begun in Spanish I. Destinos is a soap opera that allows students to learn Spanish and experience its cultural diversity while following a good story full of surprises and human emotions. Spanish II also includes additional materials, such as Spanish films and other media, various types of reading selections and online resources.
