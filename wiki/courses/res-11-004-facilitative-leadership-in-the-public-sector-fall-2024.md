# Facilitative Leadership in the Public Sector

This four-part dinner series was created by Prof. Lawrence Susskind at the request of a group of students in MIT’s Department of Urban Studies and Planning (DUSP) who felt the need for a graduate-level leadership course that would be attuned specifically to the needs of public sector professionals.
The trailer and session videos may be freely viewed, downloaded, and reused under MIT OpenCourseWare’s Creative Commons BY-NC-SA&nbsp;license.
