# RAISE (Responsible AI for Social Empowerment and Education)

RAISE&nbsp;(Responsible AI for Social Empowerment and Education) is a new MIT-wide initiative headquartered in the&nbsp;MIT Media Lab&nbsp;and in collaboration with the&nbsp;MIT Schwarzman College of Computing&nbsp;and&nbsp;MIT Open Learning. MIT researchers continually develop&nbsp;curriculum modules and associated teaching materials&nbsp;that are available to all K-12 educators for free under a Creative Commons license.
