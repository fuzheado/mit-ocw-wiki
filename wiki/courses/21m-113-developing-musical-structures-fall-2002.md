# Developing Musical Structures

The goal of this class is practical:&nbsp;to interrogate, make explicit, and thus&nbsp;to develop the powerful musical intuitions that are at work as you make sense of the music all around you.&nbsp;Reflecting, we will ask how this knowledge develops in ordinary and extraordinary ways.
