# Digital Typography

This class introduces studies in the algorithmic manipulation of type as word, symbol, and form. Problems covered will include semantic filtering, inherently unstable letterforms, and spoken letters.&nbsp;The history and traditions of typography, and their entry into the digital age, will be studied. Weekly assignments using Java®&nbsp;will explore new ways of looking at and manipulating type.
