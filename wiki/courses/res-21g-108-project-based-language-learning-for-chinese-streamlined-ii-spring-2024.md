# Project-Based Language Learning for Chinese II (Streamlined)

This is the student project for the Spring 2024 MIT course&nbsp;21G.108 Chinese II (Streamlined). In the project, Chinese heritage language students explore various topics, including the meaning of their Chinese names. Based on their discoveries, each student creates an online book.
