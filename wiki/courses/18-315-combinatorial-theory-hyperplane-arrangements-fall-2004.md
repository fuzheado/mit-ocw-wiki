# Combinatorial Theory: Hyperplane Arrangements

This is a graduate-level course in combinatorial theory.&nbsp;The content varies year to year, according to the interests of the instructor and the students.&nbsp;The topic of this course is hyperplane arrangements, including background material from the theory of posets and matroids.
