# Advanced Projects in the Visual Arts: Personal Narrative

This advanced video class serves goes into greater depth on the topics covered in 4.351 Introduction to Video.&nbsp;It also will explore the nature and function of narrative in cinema and video through exercises and screenings culminating in a final project. Starting with a brief introduction to the basic principles of classical narrative cinema, we will proceed to explore strategies designed to test the elements of narrative: story trajectory, character development, verisimilitude, time-space continuity, viewer identification, suspension of disbelief, and closure.
