# Introduction to Observational Physical Oceanography

Observational physical oceanography includes topics&nbsp;such as the &nbsp;physical description of the sea, the physical properties of seawater, methods and measurements, wind-driven ocean circulation, abyssal ocean circulation, boundary processes, and wave motions.
