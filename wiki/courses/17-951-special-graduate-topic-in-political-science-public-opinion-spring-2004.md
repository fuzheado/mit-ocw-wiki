# Special Graduate Topic in Political Science: Public Opinion

This course provides an introduction to the vast literature devoted to public opinion. In the next 12 weeks, we will survey the major theoretical approaches and empirical research&nbsp;in the field of political behavior (though we will only tangentially discuss political&nbsp; participation and voting). For the most part we will focus on American public opinion, though some of the work we will read is comparative in nature.
