# Paradox and Infinity

This course presents highlights of the more technical side of philosophy. It studies a cluster of puzzles, paradoxes, and intellectual wonders&nbsp;— from the higher infinite to Gödel’s Theorem&nbsp;— and discusses their philosophical implications.
