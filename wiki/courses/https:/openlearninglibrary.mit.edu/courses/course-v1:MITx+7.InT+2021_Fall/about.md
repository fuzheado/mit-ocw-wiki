# Inclusive Teaching Module

Welcome to the Inclusive Teaching Module! We are so glad you are joining us. The Inclusive Teaching Module is both a standalone online resource for those looking to explore materials related to inclusive teaching as well as an integral part of a blended workshop available to use at your own institution. If you are looking to facilitate a blended workshop using this material, please navigate to the Facilitation Guide and Appendix section from the course outline to get started!
