# Trace Element Analysis of Geological, Biological & Environmental Materials by Neutron Activation Analysis: An Exposure

This course introduces students to the technique of instrumental neutron activation analysis. This is a non-destructive analytical technique for the determination of elemental abundances at&nbsp;trace levels in a wide variety of geological, biological, environmental and industrial samples.
