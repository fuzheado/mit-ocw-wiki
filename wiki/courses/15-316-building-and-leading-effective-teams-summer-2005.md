# Building and Leading Effective Teams

This course is an intensive one-week introduction to leadership, teams, and learning communities. The class meets daily for&nbsp;five days. The class serves as an introduction of concepts and uses a variety of experiential exercises to develop individual and team skills, as well as supportive relationships within the Leaders for Manufacturing class.&nbsp;As part of the focus on leadership, it discusses the idea of the “Universe Within”, the images, thoughts, and experiences that are internal to all leaders.
