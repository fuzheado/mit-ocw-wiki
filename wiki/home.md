# OCW LLM Wiki

A living knowledge base of MIT OpenCourseWare's 2,500+ courses, built incrementally by an LLM agent following the [LLM Wiki](https://gist.github.com/karpathy/442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f) pattern.

This wiki catalogs every OCW course, links them to departments, topics, and instructors, and cross-references them against Wikipedia to find where MIT's open-licensed educational materials can improve the world's encyclopedia.
